FAQ

Questions, answered.

What does my client see — do they need to download an app?
Your client receives an email invite with a link and their login credentials. They access their portal through any web browser — no app download, no account creation on their end beyond the invite. It's completely frictionless for them.
What happens if I exceed the client limit on my plan?
You'll get a notification when you're at 80% capacity. You can upgrade at any time — it's prorated to the day. We never lock you out of an existing client portal without warning.
Is the free plan really free forever?
Yes. The free plan supports 1 active client portal indefinitely. The limitation is that your portal will show "Powered by Voklo" branding — if you want to remove that and use your own brand fully, you'll need a paid plan. But for freelancers just starting out, the free plan is genuinely functional.
How does the white-labelling work exactly?
On paid plans, you upload your logo, set your brand colours, and choose your subdomain (yourname.voklo.app on Solo, or your own domain like portal.yourname.com on Studio). Every email clients receive, every page they visit — it's your brand. Voklo is invisible.
How do payments work? Do you take a cut?
Payments are processed through Stripe. We take zero platform fee — you only pay Stripe's standard processing rate (typically 1.4% + 20p for European cards, 2.9% + 30¢ for US). Funds go directly to your connected bank account. HoneyBook charges an additional 3% on top of this — with Voklo, that saving adds up fast.
Can I migrate from HoneyBook or Dubsado?
We have an import tool that pulls in your client contacts and basic project history. For active projects, we recommend Voklo alongside your current tool for a month before switching — your clients won't notice any disruption. Our team is available via chat to help you migrate.
What file types are supported?
Any file type can be uploaded and downloaded. PDFs and images render as previews directly in the portal so clients can view them without downloading. Videos (MP4, MOV) stream in-portal. Source files like .fig, .ai, .psd, and .zip download directly.
